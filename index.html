Requirements for COVID-19 IVD Validation

  • You are to submit 300 worth of test kits for the validation exercise and pay requisite validation fees, via the Remita platform on our website: www.mlscn.gov.ng
  • To make payment, select ‘Public Medical Laboratory’ and describe payment as ‘Payment for Covid 19 Validation.
  • For company registration, please fill the attached form for each company and attach all necessary documents with the relevant payment. We will be glad to provide any further support to you and your team in the fight against this pandemic.
Below are applicable documentation for your use in the requesting of validation for your COVID-19 IVDs.
  • IVD Company Registration Form
  • Product listing file / dossier document submission requirements
For further clarifications/ Enquiries, please contact us via email – poffutalu@mlscn.gov.ng

Disclaimer

It has come to the attention of the Medical Laboratory Science Council of Nigeria that some individuals posing as staff of Council have adopted for use fake Council email addresses and fake letterheads (which may be close to but different from the original) to put forward request for invitations to various international conferences. The ultimate aim of these individuals is to secure unmerited visas with which to travel out of Nigeria even if it means denting the hard earned reputation of Council. The Medical Laboratory Science Council of Nigeria hereby notifies the public particularly the international community that these individuals are not bonafide staff of the Council and all such requests are fake and do not originate from Council. Any person who has any inquiry to make about such requests should contact Council via its authentic email address – info@mlscn.gov.ng Anyone who carries out any transaction with any of these imposters and fraudsters does so at his/her own risk.
